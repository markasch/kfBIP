# Bayes' Theorem

## Introduction

Bayes' theorem is at the core of modern uncertainty quantification.

:::{prf:theorem} Bayes Theorem
:nonumber:
$$
 P(a \vert b) = \frac{P(b\vert a) P(a)}{P(b)},
$$
where $P(a \vert b)$ is the conditional probability of $a$ given $b.$
:::
